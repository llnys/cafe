Welcome to Cafe Gardenia

Enjoy the laid-back atmosphere and delicious menu items of Cafe Gardenia in Barcelona. Our cafe opened in 2004, and has ever since been a place for meeting people, enjoying life and creating memories.

Fair Trade coffee

Organic farming methods guarantee that beans are grown without toxic chemicals. After harvest, the beans are roasted using sustainable methods. That is how we get quality beans, which we brew in house for a perfect cup of coffee.

Fresh, every day

To guarantee freshness and quality, we use sustainably sourced ingredients from local suppliers whenever possible. Using these ingredients we are able to make our delicious desserts, snacks, and beverages for you to enjoy. We hope to see you soon!
